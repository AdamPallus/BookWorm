# Roque

## Summary
Roque is a quiet, poetic House Mars boy who has stayed close to Darrow through much of the Institute. He has seemed gentler than the others, but Chapter 36 shows he has been caught up in the House's violence and may be badly injured.

## What We Know So Far

- He is one of the House Mars students Darrow gets to know at dinner, where he comes across as thoughtful and measured (ch. 21).
- He remains part of Darrow's smaller coalition through the fighting in the valley (chs. 24-35).
- Sevro says he has found Roque in the north, but also says he cannot move him, which suggests Roque is in serious trouble (ch. 36).

## Relationships

- **[Darrow](darrow.md)**: Close friend and fellow Mars leader.
- **[Cassius au Bellona](cassius-au-bellona.md)**: Another House Mars friend.
- **[Sevro](sevro.md)**: Sevro is the one who reports finding him.

## Open Questions

- Is Roque alive or dead?
- How badly injured is he?

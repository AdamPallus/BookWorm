# Greatwoods

## Summary
The Greatwoods are a dense forested region in the Institute where House Diana is based. The woods are full of hidden scouts, godTrees, and enough cover to make simple navigation a joke.

## Description
The Greatwoods are packed with thick shrubbery at the edges and enormous godTrees deeper in, with autumn leaves, birds, and hidden watchers everywhere. Darrow compares them to a city and to his mine at the same time (ch. 33).

## Significance
This is the setting for Darrow's first diplomatic attempt with another House. The terrain favors concealment, misdirection, and ambush rather than open fighting (ch. 33).
